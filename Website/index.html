Little planet

A fast-paced minigame made in 6 hours.

Inspired by the famous youtuber "Brackeys" I wanted to make a look-a-like of the Ludum-Dare game "Shrinking Planet" I chose to do this because I wanted to challenge myself in making a game that required coding techniques I didn't know yet. I also wanted to test how I perform under stress with a very tight deadline. I'm very happy with the result and it was a great project.

The Stack

A simple mobile game where you build the biggest stack possible.

Upon seeing the official version of "The Stack" I wanted to try and replicate it from scratch to test my mathematical progamming skills. The math behind the whole placement of pieces of towers proved difficult. But willing to do research I managed to find solutions to these problems very quickly. Overall this was a great little project which taught me alot about the mathematical part of programming.
